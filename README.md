## ğŸ¯ Usage

1. **Creating a Board**
   - Click the "Create Board" button
   - Enter a board title
   - Click "Create"

2. **Adding Lists**
   - Click "Add List" on your board
   - Enter a list title
   - Click "Add"

3. **Adding Cards**
   - Click "Add Card" on any list
   - Enter card details
   - Click "Add"

4. **Moving Cards**
   - Drag and drop cards between lists
   - Cards will automatically update their position
